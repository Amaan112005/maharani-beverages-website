Shipping & Delivery Governance

1. Regional Logistics & Coverage

Maharani Beverages LLP provides strategic delivery services for Zenith Packaged Drinking Water across **Kolkata** and flanking metropolitan regions. Deliveries to locations outside standard service archetypes are reserved for bulk and institutional acquisitions, subject to operational feasibility and prior high-level confirmation.

2. Order Classification Model

Zenith logistical operations are categorized into two primary tiers:

  • Standard Orders: Regular high-frequency residential and small-volume unit fulfillment.
  • Bulk / Institutional: High-volume deployment for corporate offices, premiere events, and commercial sanctuaries.

3. Processing & Dispatch Governance

Every order undergoes a multi-stage validation cycle prior to dispatch:

  • Inventory Availability Synchronization.
  • Address Verification & Geocoding.
  • Final Dispatch Coordination.
Operations are conducted during established business windows. Direct contact may be initiated for logistical alignment before the vehicle leaves the hub.

4. Temporal Logistics Windows

  • Standard Fulfillment: 24 to 48 hours from official confirmation.
  • Institutional Volume: Timelines for bulk orders are calibrated based on quantity and site complexity.
Precise delivery windows will be communicated during the validation phase.

5. Value-Based Logistics Pricing

Delivery investment is calculated via our precision metrics:

  • Unit count and total architectural weight.
  • Distance from our central fulfillment hub.
  • Institutional Priority Agreements.
*Complimentary delivery may be native to recurring elite plans and specific bulk volumes.*

6. Precision Coordination & Scheduling

For large-scale institutional deliveries, absolute scheduling is required. Verified time slots are generated to ensure non-disruptive deployment. Customers are expected to ensure the receiving site is architected for immediate intake to avoid operational fatigue.

7. Point of Transfer Audit

The moment of delivery represents the official point of transfer. Customers are required to perform a physical audit at the site. Once accepted without recorded objection, the logistical cycle is considered successful and responsibility for the units transfers to the customer.

8. Unsuccessful Logistics Incidents

A delivery is marked as "Unsuccessful" under the following conditions:

  • Absence of receiving authority.
  • Invalid geodata or contact metrics.
  • Refusal of intake without pharmacological justification.
*Secondary attempts may incur architectural penalty fees.*

9. Operational Variance

While Zeniths strives for chronological perfection, external factors like atmospheric conditions, regional congestion, or systemic surges may impact timing. Our logistics desk will maintain active communication in such events.

10. Institutional Purity Agreements

Hotels, corporate headquarters, and distribution hubs are encouraged to establish long-term supply agreements. This unlocks priority scheduling, custom routing profiles, and dedicated logistical support.

11. Post-Delivery Stewardship

Subsequent to delivery, the customer is the primary steward of the product. Proper storage (shielded from heat and contamination) is mandatory. Zenith is not liable for purity degradation due to improper site stewardship.
