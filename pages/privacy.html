Overview of Purity

At Zenith, operated by Maharani Beverages LLP, we believe that privacy is as essential as purity. This Privacy Governance document outlines our absolute commitment to protecting the digital identity of our partners, distributors, and customers.

Information Acquisition

We only acquire information that is technically necessary to provide elite hydration solutions. This includes:

  • Inquiry Data: Information provided during distribution or bulk hydration requests.
  • Network Logistics: Technical data required for supply chain precision.
  • Communication: Direct correspondence via our WhatsApp and Portal channels.

Structural Security

Your data is shielded using redundant, high-tensile encryption protocols. We do not engage in the commercial exchange of your personal information. Our data mandate is built on the same "Human-Free" logic as our purification engine—isolated and secure.
