Frequently Asked Questions

Do you supply water to restaurants, cafés, fast-food outlets, and cloud kitchens in Howrah?
Yes, Zenith Water provides FSSAI-certified packaged drinking water specifically for restaurants, cafés, fast-food outlets, and cloud kitchens across Howrah.
What bottle sizes do you recommend for restaurants?
For restaurants, we recommend 500ml, 1L, and 2L bottles plus 20L jars for kitchen and dispenser use. Custom combinations are available on request.
Can you handle large-volume orders for restaurants?
Yes, we supply palletized bulk orders and recurring 20L jar programs suitable for restaurants.
Is same-day delivery available for restaurants, cafés, fast-food outlets, and cloud kitchens in Howrah?
Same-day delivery may be available for central Howrah locations; standard delivery is 24–48 hours.
Do you provide GST invoices and compliance documents?
Yes, every B2B delivery includes GST invoices, FSSAI license details, and batch traceability.
How do I set up a recurring supply contract?
Contact us with your monthly volume and schedule; we will draft a supply agreement with fixed delivery windows.
Is the water safe for patients and children?
Yes, our 7-stage purification and sealed packaging make it safe for all age groups and sensitive settings.
Do you cover both Kolkata and Howrah?
Yes, we deliver across Kolkata and Howrah from our Ranihati manufacturing facility.
What is the shelf life of Zenith bottled water?
Sealed bottles are best consumed before the expiry date printed on the cap, typically 6 months from packing.
Can I get a free sample for my premises?
Yes, sample bottles or jars can be arranged for qualified business enquiries in Kolkata and Howrah.
