Why Choose Zenith in Dum Dum

Dum Dum is one of Kolkata's most strategically vital zones. With the Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport anchoring its northern edge, and the Dum Dum Metro junction connecting the Blue and Yellow lines, this area sees an endless flow of travellers, commuters, and residents. The density of hotels near the airport, apartment blocks along Jessore Road, and commercial hubs near the Cantonment make Dum Dum a high-priority zone for dependable water supply.

Zenith Water, produced at our Ranihati facility in Howrah, bridges the gap with rapid north Kolkata logistics. We understand that airport hotels cannot afford to run out of stock, that Metro-area offices need bottles ready before the morning rush, and that families in the residential pockets deserve the same premium quality as five-star guests.

Our Dum Dum delivery operation runs six days a week with early-morning loading and GPS-tracked routing. Every case of Zenith water is sealed, dated, and transported in hygienic conditions from our plant to your premises. For bulk consumers, we assign dedicated account managers who monitor your stock levels and preemptively schedule refills.
