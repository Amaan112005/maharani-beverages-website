The Process

Source to Sip.

Our process follows strong hygiene norms and safety standards.

Artesian Source

We draw water from Bengal's protected groundwater reserves. Rigorous source monitoring keeps the mineral profile steady and ensures biological safety.

Molecular Filtration

Seven stages work in sequence. They capture sediment, filter with activated carbon, remove dissolved solids through reverse osmosis at 0.0001 micron, sterilize with UV, and treat with ozone.

Precision Sealing

We bottle under ISO-certified conditions. Ozone-lock technology keeps the water fresh without any chemical preservatives.
